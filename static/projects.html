Tricked Out Timeline

Tricked Out Timeline

Tricked Out Timeline is a webapp that allows anyone to make cool cover photos for their Facebook Timelines.

There are multiple effects, such as merging your cover photo and profile photo and making your profile look like it has been torn off.

The site was launched in February 2012 and gained over 20K Facebook likes.

PublshMe

PublshMe

PublshMe was born in summer 2011 out of a vision of democratic journalism. It is a platform to allow independent writers and bloggers to come together and publish their work to a newspaper.

The newspapers are unedited - instead the community of readers are the ones who determine what the front page looks like.

Crewdater             

Crewdater

Find, date, rate, repeat. Crewdater was the first foray in to web development.

Crewdater is a social network that takes the age old Oxbridge tradition online. It allows crews to signup and create a profile, then find and request dates with other crews. The whole thing can be arranged on the website, and your date can be reviewed and score. Exclusive Crewdater vouchers are also issued to crewdates organised through the site to get money off restaurants for their dates.

The site gained notoriety in Oxford and is widely used. The bright pink flyers were a big hit...
