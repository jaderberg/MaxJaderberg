How to hide Facebook.com email address on profile

25 June 2012

Facebook have decided to automatically replace everyone's normal email address on their profile with a Facebook email address (yourname@facebook.com) without your permission. This means that someone trying to email you will just end up sending you a Facebook message, which is not what you want.

It is very simple however to change it back to hide the @facebook.com email and show your normal email address.

First click on the "about" box on your profile.

In your "Contact Info" you will see my @facebook.com email is displayed automatically without me wanting it there.

Click "Edit" and then ensure that you select "Hidden from Timeline" next to your @facebook.com email and "Shown on Timeline" next to your actual email address.

Now click "Save" at the bottom of the form!

All done! Now you have your regular email address back on show and avoid your emails going to your Facebook inbox.
